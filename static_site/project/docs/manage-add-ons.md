It is required to get the extension to your document before managing add-ons.  

**To get the add-on in Google Docs**:

1. Click **Extensions** in the menu.

![Image title](../image/add-on.png)

2. Click the **Add-ons** list. Then click **Get add-ons**. 

![Image title](../image/add-on2.png)

3. Fill in the **Search apps** field with the add-on’s name. 

![Image title](../image/add-on3.png)

4. Click the necessary add-on. Then click **Install** to get the add-on to your document. 

![Image title](../image/add-on5.png)

5. Give the necessary permissions to use the add-on in your Google account. Then click **Done** to finish the add-on’s installation.

![Image title](../image/add-on6.png)

The add-on is added to your document in Google Docs. 

**To manage add-ons in Google Docs**:

1. Click **Extensions** in the menu.  

![Image title](../image/extension.png)

2. Click **Add-ons**. Then click **Manage add-ons** to observe available add-ons in your Google Docs. 

![Image title](../image/extension2.png)

3. Click <img src="/image/new10.png" class="emoji-icon"> **Options** next to the add-on name. Then choose the necessary options for add-on managing: 

![Image title](../image/extension3.png)

A. Click **Uninstall** to remove the add-on from your Google Docs. Then click **Uninstall app**. 

![Image title](../image/installation.png)

B. Click **Use in this document** to use the add-on from the **Extensions** menu in your document.  


![Image title](../image/insert_icons.png)

- Click **Insert icons for Docs**. Then click **Open sidebar to select icons** to open the sidebar with the add-on. 

![Image title](../image/insert_icons2.png)

 The sidebar with the add-on is opened in your document. 

 C. Click **Help** to get additional information about your add-on:

  ![Image title](../image/help.png)

- Click **View in store** to get information about the add-on’s overview, permissions, and reviews. 

- Click **Report an issue** to describe the problems with your add-on by e-mail. 

D. Click **Report an issue** to describe the problems with your add-on by e-mail. Then click **Send**.


![Image title](../image/send.png)

Your add-on is managed.
