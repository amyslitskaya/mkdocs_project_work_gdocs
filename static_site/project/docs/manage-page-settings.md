**To manage the page settings in Google Docs**:

1. Click **File** in the menu. 

![Image title](../image/settings.png)

2. Click **Page setup** to adjust settings to the document layout:

![Image title](../image/settings2.png)

A. Click **Pages** to apply the **Orientation**, **Paper size**, **Page color**, **Margins** options to the whole document. Then click **OK**.

![Image title](../image/setup.png)

B. Click **Pageless** to apply the **Background color** option and the **Pageless** option to the document. Then click **OK**. 

![Image title](../image/setup2.png)

The page settings are managed.
