It is recommended to control the persons who view, comment, or edit your document in their actions. 

**To manage access in Google Docs**:

1. Open your document in Google Docs.   
1. Click **Share**. 

![Image title](../image/manage.png)

3. Fill out the **Share** form:

![Image title](../image/manage2.png)

A. Click **Pages** to apply the **Orientation**, **Paper size**, **Page color**, **Margins** options to the whole document. Then click **OK**.

Click Pageless to apply the Background color option and the Pageless option to the document. Then click OK. 

B. Click **Pageless** to apply the **Background color** option and the **Pageless** option to the document. Then click **OK**. 

![Image title](../image/setup2.png)

The page settings are managed.
