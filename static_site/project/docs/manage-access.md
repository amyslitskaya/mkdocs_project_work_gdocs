It is recommended to control the persons who view, comment, or edit your document in their actions. 

**To manage access in Google Docs**:

1. Open your document in Google Docs.   
1. Click **Share**. 

![Image title](../image/manage.png)

3. Fill out the **Share** form:

![Image title](../image/manage2.png)

A. Type the email addresses of the necessary people in the **Add people, groups, and calendar events** field. 

![Image title](../image/access.png)

B. Click the **Editor** list to choose the permission level:

![Image title](../image/access2.png)

- Click **Viewer** to allow the person to view the document.

- Click **Commenter** to enable the person to view and comment on the document.

- Click **Editor** to allow the person to make changes to the document.

C. Click the **Restricted** list to choose the link access level:

![Image title](../image/access3.png)

- Click **Restricted** to restrict access to your document for people without the link. 

- Click **Anyone with the link** to share access to your document on the Internet. Then click the **Viewer** list to choose the permission level: 

![Image title](../image/access4.png)

- Click **Viewer** to allow the person to view the document.

- Click **Commenter** to enable the person to view and comment on the document. 

- Click **Editor** to allow the person to make changes to the document. 

D. Click <img src="/image/new8.png" class="emoji-icon"> **Settings** to manage permissions for the viewer, commenter, and editor roles: 

![Image title](../image/access5.png)

- Select the **Editors can change permissions and share** checkbox to restrict sharing the document and inviting other people.

- Select the **Viewers and commenters can see the option to download, print, and copy** checkbox to restrict to download, print, and copy the document.   

E. Click <img src="/image/new5.png" class="emoji-icon"> **Copy link** to copy the document to your clipboard.

F. Click **Done** to save the changes in the **Share** form. 

![Image title](../image/access6.png)

Access to the documentation is managed.
