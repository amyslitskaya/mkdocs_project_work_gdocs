It is required to select the text you want to change before formatting the text in Google Docs. 

**To change font**: 

1. Click **Font**.

![Image title](../image/format.png) 

2. Click **Font** in the list.

![Image title](../image/format2.png)

The necessary font is applied to your text. 

**To change font size**: 

- Click **Decrease font size** to decrease the text size. 

![Image title](../image/format3.png)

- Click **Font size**. Then click the necessary number in the list.

![Image title](../image/fontsize1.png)

- Click **Increase font size** to increase the text size. 

![Image title](../image/format5.png)

The necessary font size is applied to your text.

**To change font type**: 

- Click **Bold**, **Italic**, or **Underlined**. 

![Image title](../image/format6.png)

Your text is displayed in the corresponding font type. 

**To change text color**:

1. Click **Text color**.

![Image title](../image/format7.png)

2. Click the necessary color from the palette. 

![Image title](../image/format8.png)

Your text color is changed. 

**To change highlight color**: 

1. Click **Highlight color**.  

![Image title](../image/format9.png)

2. Click the necessary color from the palette.

![Image title](../image/format10.png)

Your text is highlighted.
