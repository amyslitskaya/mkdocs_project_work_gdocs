It is required to connect the printer to your device before printing the document. 

**To print the document**:

1. Click **File** in the menu.

![Image title](../image/print.png)

2. Click **Print**. 

![Image title](../image/print2.png)

3. Choose the necessary setting for the printer on the sidebar. 

![Image title](../image/print3.png)

4. Click **Print**.

Your document is printed.
